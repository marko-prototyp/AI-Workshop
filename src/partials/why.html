At some point in our careers we stop making things and start managing the process of making things. Tickets, briefs, Figma comments, client feedback. Everything interesting gets sanded down to nothing. None of us got into design for that part.

AI won't fix your job. But it will let you build your own thing, for yourself, just because you want to. No waiting on a developer, no approval chain, no "we'll do it in Q3." You have an idea on Tuesday and something real by Thursday.

That's what this workshop is. Twelve hours to remember the part of the job you came here for.
