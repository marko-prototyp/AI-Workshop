After session facilitator notes: I have new findings regarding this session. Since we were all pretty bummed how Claude design didnt really deliver a good design, I went researching to find the best way to produce what we want. The best way to go is using "/skills ". More accurately, creating a skill that references the visual style you want. For our purposes we took a skill template from Material design which has pretty much everyting defined, and adapted it to our wanted design. We did the customization by providing image references to the model, and it took the inspiration and rewrote the Material skill do suit our needs. After that I got a skill that fits Paula's desired product. Using the new skill I got to create 2 screens in almost perfect sync with what I told it to do. 

So this is probably the best way to go, and I should stop and rethink the future and past steps of this workshop to accommodate this new way of working.
