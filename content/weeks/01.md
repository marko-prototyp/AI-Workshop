num: "01"
title: "Meet Claude. Pick your project."
phase: "Discover"
capability: "Prompting fundamentals; conversation as a thinking tool"
toolkit: "Sonnet in Claude.ai chat. Conversational intake; speed over depth."
exercise:
  - "20-minute interview where Claude asks the designer about strengths, ambitions, energy."
  - "Generate 3 project directions matched to them."
  - "Pick one. Draft the brief by end of session."
prompts:
  - title: "The intake interview"
    body: |
      I'm a UI/UX designer joining a 12-week program where I'll build a personal project using AI. Before I pick what to build, I want you to interview me. Ask me one question at a time about: what I'm strongest at, what I want to be better known for, what kind of work energizes me vs. drains me, and what audience I'd want this project to reach. After about 8–10 questions, summarize what you've learned about me in 5 bullet points and stop.
  - title: "Generate project directions"
    body: |
      Based on the summary you just made of me, suggest 3 project directions I could build in 12 weeks at 1–2 hours per week. The options are: portfolio site, interactive case study, "tiny tool", playful microsite, mini design system. For each, tell me: what it would look like, why it fits me specifically, and one risk. Be honest — don't just be encouraging.
outputs:
  - "A self-summary"
  - "A chosen direction"
  - "A first-draft brief"
watch:
  - "Don't let people pick the first idea Claude proposes"
  - "'A portfolio site' isn't specific enough — push for 'who is this for?'"
