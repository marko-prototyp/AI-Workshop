We built this session with two paths. Comp the screen in Figma and hand the screenshot to Claude, or describe it straight to Claude Design. Nobody chose Figma. Every designer went to Claude Design, and what came back held the brief better than the same prompt did in a normal chat. So the session follows them. Figma still works, and the idea behind it is the same: design first, then build. But the path we teach now is the one the room actually took.
