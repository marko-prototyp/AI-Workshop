  - title: "Structured comparison"
    body: |
      I'm researching [project type]. Here are 5 examples I've found inspiring: [list with URLs]. Build me a comparison table with columns: name, what it does well, what's distinctive, what NOT to copy, one specific technique I could borrow. Be specific — vague answers are useless.
  - title: "Challenge assumptions"
    body: |
      Here's my project brief: [paste]. Without being polite, push back on it. What assumptions am I making that might be wrong? What's the strongest case for doing the opposite of what I've planned?
outputs:
  - "Comparison matrix"
  - "Pushed-back assumptions"
  - "One-page research summary"
